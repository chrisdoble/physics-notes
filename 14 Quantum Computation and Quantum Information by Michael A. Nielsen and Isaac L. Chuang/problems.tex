\subsection*{Exercise 2.2}

\[A = \begin{bmatrix}
    0 & 1 \\
    1 & 0
  \end{bmatrix}\]

Using the basis $\ket{+} = (\ket{0} + \ket{1}) / \sqrt{2}$ and $\ket{-} = (\ket{0} - \ket{1}) / \sqrt{2}$ we get

\begin{align*}
  \ket{0}                                          & = \frac{\ket{+} + \ket{-}}{\sqrt{2}} \\
  \ket{1}                                          & = \frac{\ket{+} - \ket{-}}{\sqrt{2}} \\
  \begin{bmatrix}
    a_{0 0} & a_{0 1} \\
    a_{1 0} & a_{1 1}
  \end{bmatrix} \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \begin{bmatrix}
                                     1 \\
                                     1
                                   \end{bmatrix} & = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \begin{bmatrix}
                                                                          1 \\
                                                                          -1
                                                                        \end{bmatrix}    \\
  a_{0 0} + a_{0 1}                                & = 1                                  \\
  a_{1 0} + a_{1 1}                                & = -1                                 \\
  \begin{bmatrix}
    a_{0 0} & a_{0 1} \\
    a_{1 0} & a_{1 1}
  \end{bmatrix} \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \begin{bmatrix}
                                     1 \\
                                     -1
                                   \end{bmatrix} & = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \begin{bmatrix}
                                                                          1 \\
                                                                          1
                                                                        \end{bmatrix}    \\
  a_{0 0} - a_{0 1}                                & = 1                                  \\
  a_{1 0} - a_{1 1}                                & = 1                                  \\
  A                                                & = \begin{bmatrix}
                                                         1 & 0  \\
                                                         0 & -1
                                                       \end{bmatrix}
\end{align*}
